Transcription Notes

  • æ (ash) → certh 48 (a) + certh 46 (e), written as digraph
  • hw → certh 5 (dedicated /hw/ rune)
  • cw → certh 23 (dedicated /kw/ rune)
  • þ/ð (thorn/eth) → certh 10 (voiceless th) / certh 11 (voiced dh)
  • ge- prefix → certh 14 (/j/) + vowel (palatalized in OE)
  • sc → certh 15 (/ʃ/, as in OE "scip" = ship)
  • Word separator: certh space dot (\ in font)
  • Sentence punctuation: three dots (P in font)
  • Mode: Angerthas Daeron values (baseline for OE/Rohirric transcription)
