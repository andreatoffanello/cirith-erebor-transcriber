Phonological Rules Applied

  • c before front vowels (e, i, æ, y) → /tʃ/ certh 15 (palatal); before back vowels → /k/ certh 18
  • g initial + front vowel → /j/ certh 14 (palatal); post-back-vowel → /ɣ/ certh 21 (fricative); post-front-vowel → /j/ certh 40
  • f intervocalic → /v/ certh 4; elsewhere → /f/ certh 3
  • s intervocalic → /z/ certh 35; elsewhere → /s/ certh 34
  • þ intervocalic → /ð/ certh 11 (voiced); elsewhere → /θ/ certh 10. ð always → certh 11
  • hw → certh 5 · cw → certh 23 · sc → certh 15 · ng → certh 36 · nd → certh 33
  • hl, hr, hn → h + consonant (OE voiceless sonorant clusters)
  • æ → certh 48+46 (a+e digraph) · ea, eo → diphthong pairs
  • Mode: Angerthas Daeron baseline with Erebor reversions (C12=n, C29=r, C34=s, C54=h)
